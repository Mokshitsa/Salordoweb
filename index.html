The Problem
Healthcare can't deploy AI.
Here's why.
Three structural barriers prevent every hospital from using AI today.
⊘

Data Fragmentation

Clinical data lives across EHRs, PACS, labs, insurance systems, and devices — none of which communicate properly. This causes incomplete records, manual transfers, and medical errors.

⚠

Unsafe AI Deployment

No monitoring, no auditability, no clinical validation layer, no human override. AI agents today operate entirely outside clinical environments with zero guardrails.

◈

Compliance & Regulatory Risk

Healthcare AI must comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and FDA regulations. Hospitals lack the technical infrastructure to enforce any of this at the AI layer.

Setup Guide
How to connect your custom domain
A simple step-by-step guide to get salordo.com (or any domain) pointing to your hosted site.
1

Buy your domain name

Go to a domain registrar and purchase your domain (e.g. salordo.com). Popular registrars include:

Namecheap GoDaddy Google Domains Cloudflare Porkbun
2

Choose where to host your site

Upload this HTML file to a hosting platform. The easiest free options for a landing page like this:

Vercel (recommended) Netlify Cloudflare Pages GitHub Pages

For Vercel: Sign up at vercel.com → drag and drop your HTML file → it deploys instantly and gives you a URL like your-project.vercel.app

3

Connect your domain to the host

In your hosting dashboard (e.g. Vercel), go to Settings → Domains and add salordo.com. It will give you DNS records to set up — usually something like:

A record pointing to 76.76.21.21
or a CNAME record pointing to cname.vercel-dns.com

4

Update your DNS settings

Go back to your domain registrar (where you bought the domain). Find DNS Settings or DNS Management, then add the records your host gave you. It usually takes 5–30 minutes for the changes to go live (sometimes up to 48 hours).

5

Enable HTTPS (automatic)

Most modern hosts like Vercel, Netlify, and Cloudflare Pages automatically set up a free SSL certificate for your domain. Once DNS is connected, your site will be live at https://salordo.com with the padlock icon.

💡 Quickest path (under 15 minutes)

Buy domain on Namecheap (~$10/year) → Deploy this file on Vercel (free) → Add domain in Vercel settings → Update nameservers on Namecheap → Done. If you want someone to do it for you, any freelance web developer on Fiverr or Upwork can set this up in under an hour for $20–50.
